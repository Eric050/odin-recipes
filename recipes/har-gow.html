Har Gow

Description

A classic dim sum dish served as steam crystal shrimp dumplings and is considered one of the classic and most served dish in chinese resturants

Ingredients

Core ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pork Fat
  • Bamboo Shoots

Wrapper ingredients

  • Wheat
  • Corn
  • Tapioca
  • Starch
  • Oil

Recipe steps

  1. Cut up some of the prawns into chunks. If you have small-ish prawns, keep them whole. If using large ones, cut them in half or thirds. Cut enough chunks so that there is 1 piece per dumpling.
  2. Blend the chunks with bamboo shoots and seasoning
  3. Place prawn paste into a mixing bowl and mix with prawn chunks
  4. Cover the mixture and place in the refrigerator
  5. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the wheat starch, tapioca starch and salt together with a pair of chopsticks (or a fork). Then, add the oil and BOILING hot water.
  6. Very quickly, mix the dough mixture. It should solidify and turn into a rough dough
  7. take one piece of dumpling dough, roll it into a round sphere with the palm of your hands then place and press the dough onto the work surface into a disk. Then, roll the dough into a round translucent skin or sheet, turning regularly to keep it evenly round.
  8. Repeat for the rest
  9. Steam the dumplings
