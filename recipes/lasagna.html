Lasagna

Description

Originating in Italy, lasagne has been influential and popular in not only italian cusine but internationally too.

Lasagna is a dish that consists of layers of pasta stacked on top of each other with fillings alternating between stacks. Fillings include cheese, meats, tomato sauce and among many others. Here, we outline the steps in making this delicious recipe

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Parsley
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta cheese
  • An egg

Recipe steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
